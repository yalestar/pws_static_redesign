Sponsor Portland Water Spectacular

Sponsoring Portland Water Spectacular is fun, easy, and a fantastic way to get unique exposure for your business. We're happy to work with you to determine the best fit for your needs. Please contact us with any questions.

Downloadable PDF version of this information

Here are the current sponsorship options:

Presenting Sponsor: $5000

  • Having the team introduced at every show as "Portland Water Spectacular presented by Sponsor Name"
  • The sponsor’s logo on the sign at the entrance of the team’s home ski site
  • Company banner on Center Stage
  • Company banner on Jump Ramp
  • Company banner on Show Tent Full page ad in official Show Program
  • Company logo on front page of the official Show Program Sponsor's Information/samples distributed at event in show tent
  • Sponsorship logo on team's website home page hyperlinked to company's website
  • The sponsor’s recorded commercial played pre and post show during every regular show
  • Sponsor recognition throughout the event.
  • Listed as "Presented by" with logo on all show Flyers distributed around town and at show sites
  • Sponsor recognition in pre- event promotion including social media and press releases
  • Includes printing of all banners

Major Sponsor: $2000

  • Company banner on Jump Ramp, Pickup Boat OR Main Show Area (on a first-come basis)
  • Company banner on Show Tent
  • Sponsor's information/samples distributed at the event in the show tent
  • The sponsor’s verbal acknowledgement announced during every regular show
  • A half-page advertisement in the official Show Program Company logo on "Sponsors" page of team's website hyperlinked to company's website
  • Sponsor recognition in pre- event promotion including social media and press releases
  • Small logo on Show Flyers to be distributed around town and at show sites

Supporting Sponsor: $1000

  • Company banner on site in a high-profile feature area
  • The sponsor’s verbal acknowledgement announced during every regular show
  • The sponsor’s half page advertisement in the team’s program
  • The sponsor’s logo on a billboard at information booth
  • Company logo on "Sponsors" page of team's website hyperlinked to company's website
  • Sponsor recognition in pre-event promotion including social media and press releases

Ski Site Signage (per event): $250-$350

The team can place your company’s business logo on a sign at the ski site. Placement varies by location (bleachers, tent, fence, etc.) Typical size is 3’ by 4’.

  • $250 for a banner provided by the advertiser
  • $350 for a banner provided by the PWS

Program Book: $20 to $500
