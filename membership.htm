Portland Water Spectacular Wants YOU as a member!

PWS is always looking for new members!

No Water Ski Experience is Required! We are truly a family club with skiing members from five years old to over 80! The only thing required is a commitment to have fun, learn, and give it your best. PWS membership is not limited to just skiers. We're always looking for skiers and support personnel such as boat drivers, boat riders/spotters, actors for shore routines, announcers and sound-system personnel, and costume designers.

We will teach you what it takes to become a:

  • Barefooter
  • Jumper
  • Pyramid Skier
  • Swivel Skier
  • Shoe Skier
  • Boat Driver / Observer
  • And More!
