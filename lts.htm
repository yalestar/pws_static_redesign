Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to bring?

You only need to bring a bathing suit (and ideally a towel to dry off with afterwards).  The team will have all of the skis and floatation that you will need.  Of course if you have any equipment that you want to use then please feel free to bring it with you.

 

Do I need to RSVP?

It is helpful to make sure there is room..
