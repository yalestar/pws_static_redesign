PWS is always looking for new members!

No waterski experience is required! We're truly a family club with skiing members from five years old to over 80! The only thing required is a commitment to have fun, learn, and give it your best. PWS membership is not limited to just skiers. We're always looking for skiers and support personnel such as boat drivers, boat riders/spotters, actors for shore routines, announcers and sound-system personnel, and costume designers.

We will teach you what it takes to become a:

  • Barefooter
  • Jumper
  • Pyramid Skier
  • Swivel Skier
  • Shoe Skier
  • Boat Driver / Observer
  • And More!

Summer Practice Schedule:

Tuesday 6pm - Dusk Girls' Practice
Wednesday 6pm - Dusk Full Team
Sunday 10am - 1pm Full Team
Our home practice site is on the Willamette River in West Linn, OR, just above the Willamette Falls. Practices are held every Sunday at 10:00 AM from April through October.

Winter Dryland Practice Schedule

February through April we typically practice in a gym at Crossroads Church (2505 NE 102nd Ave, Portland, OR 97220) every other Sunday from 1:00pm to 3:00pm.
